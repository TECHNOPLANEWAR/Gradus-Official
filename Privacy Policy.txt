This Privacy Policy is designed to inform users about the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information related to the usage of the Gradus app. By choosing to use Gradus, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy. The terminology used in this Privacy Policy aligns with the definitions provided in the Terms and Conditions, also available on the website and in the Gradus app.

Information Collection and Use
In order to utilize the features of the Gradus app, students are required to provide their gradebook system login credentials. This information, along with any other data displayed in the app, is stored solely on your device and is neither collected nor accessible by the Gradus team. The app utilizes the credentials provided to retrieve and display information directly from your gradebook system server, ensuring that the Gradus team has no access to the data accessed through the app.

Log Data
During app usage, if a crash occurs, log data from your device will be sent to the Play Store or App Store servers. This Log Data may include details such as device name, operating system version, usage timestamp, and other non-sensitive statistics.

Security
As all personal information accessed through Gradus is facilitated by your school district's HAC servers, the security of this app is equivalent to that of your HAC website.
